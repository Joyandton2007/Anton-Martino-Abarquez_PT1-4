About Me

My mission is to spread awareness about the importance of self-care. For some people it could be as simple as spending time outdoors or a massage. For others, it's playing a sport. In this busy and changing world self-care is often neglected and some see it as a selfish act. It is time we change that perspective and view self-care in its true meaning, which is actively taking care of your mental and physical health, your mind, body and spirit in whatever form you feel best. Join me in my journey and quest in making self-care everyone's priority.
